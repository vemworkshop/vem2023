Keynote

Ricardo Britto

Ericsson BSS Incubator

Data Science for Software Development Acceleration

We produce data whenever we commit code, create test cases, analyze new requirements, etc. This data can be used to accelerate/improve software development itself. This talk will present how data science (e.g., statistics, machine learning) can be leveraged in software development, presenting a few use cases from Ericsson.

About the Speaker

I am a very passionate business-oriented technology leader who believes that data can make a difference.
With a Ph.D. in the area of Software Engineering and a background as a data scientist and applied researcher, I have designed, developed, and led the development of AI and Analytics solutions in Ericsson. I have extensive experience in leading Data Science teams.
I currently lead the Ericsson BSS Incubator, where we use data science and AI in innovative ways to make our BSS portfolio more attractive (AI-empowered portfolio) and optimize our R&D process.
I'm also an adjunct lecturer at the Department of Software Engineering of BTH, supervising research projects that focus on data science and AI to accelerate software development.
